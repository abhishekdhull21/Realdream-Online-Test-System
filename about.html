About Us

realdream.online is an online education and career portal. This is a single place for students and job seekers. The main aim of realdream.online is to provide students and job seekers a common platform to study and prepare for various exams. This is a complete online exam preparation hub for all competitive exams in India as well as in some other countries.
realdream.online has three main sections, Online Study, Exam Preparation and Online Test Series.
Online Study: The online study section of our website includes study material for various competitive and scholar exams. This section offers free study material for Aptitude, Reasoning, General Knowledge, Verbal Ability, Engineering, Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics and Biology subjects of various exams. Each subject includes questions and answers and practice tests for various topics.
Exam Preparation: The exam preparation section has been designed keeping in mind all popular recruitment and entrance exams. Whether it is JEE entrance examination or IBPS recruitment exam, you can find helpful exam preparation material and sample tests here. This section covers all major Government recruitment exams, Engineering exams, Entrance exams, Medical exams, Defence exams, Management exams and Study Abroad exams.
Online Test Series: Online test series is the best part of realdream.online. This is the thing that makes us different from all other online education portals. This section offers you online test series and mock tests for all major competitive exams. You can not only study online here, but can also practice with these test series designed for each and every topic from various subjects. Online test series at REAL DREAM has been designed by our subject experts maintaining the highest level of quality and accuracy.
There are available both free and paid test series here. Free test series has some limitations over paid one. Students get many benefits with the paid subscription of our test series including reward points, 24/7 customer care service, access to each and every section of our website, etc.
Online study material and Online Test Series at Real Dream is the best in every aspect including best content, best study material, best quality, best layout, and easy to understand. Another benefit of becoming a RealDream member is the Reward Points that are offered at each and every action of the user including Signup, Sign In, test attempts, profile completion, referring others to join, writing testimonials, writing reviews, etc. We guarantee 100% authentic and quality study material to our users and our customer care service is available 24 hours every day to resolve your issues.
